Privacy Policy for MoneyTalk
Last Updated: December 26, 2024

At MoneyTalk, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use
Camera Access
We request access to your device's camera to allow you to capture and upload profile pictures. Images are processed locally and stored on our secure servers (Supabase) to personalize your account experience. We do not use your camera for any other purpose.

Microphone Access
We request microphone access for our Voice Transaction feature. This allows you to input financial data hands-free. Audio is processed via Google's Speech-to-Text API for the purpose of transaction extraction only and is not recorded or stored by us.

Personal Data
We collect email addresses and names for authentication purposes via Supabase Auth. Your financial data is encrypted and stored securely within your private account.

2. Third-Party Services
We use the following services to provide our app:

Supabase: For database and authentication.
Firebase (Google): For crash reporting (Crashlytics) and notifications.
Gemini AI (Google): For transaction data extraction.
3. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights
You can delete your account or modify your data at any time through the app settings.

5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at your organization's email address.
